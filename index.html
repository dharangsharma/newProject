Our Solutions

KelTech IoT provides smart and secure Networking and IoT solutions for the Global Property Technology Market. We work with clients to support their initial Ideation of their Property Technology Vision, all the way through to Project Execution and Delivery. Clients typically include those involved in the Multi-Tenanted Commercial and Residential Property Sector such as Build to Rent, Hospitality and Student Accommodation. Our solutions are equally as applicable in the New Build as they are in the Retrofit market.
